Philosophy

A receipt does not speak to a reader — it re-executes. Hand its command to a fresh machine and a faithful receipt regenerates; if it does not reproduce, it self-denounces. That property, not its prose, is what makes a number trustworthy. No hype, no vague benchmark claims, and never a single speed score — only the delta, the curve, the gate, carrying their coordinates.

"Boring" is institutionalized suspicion, and suspicion needs a body. The ladder runs from the noob who downloaded a GGUF and ran one command, up to the expert validating a branch under load — each rung reproducible by the one below. We never skip steps to look advanced. Skipping the floor is the opposite of boring.
